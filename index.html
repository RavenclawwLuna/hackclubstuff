Budgies

How to care for you fluffy pet❤️

Cage

𝐒𝐢𝐳𝐞: A spacious cage very important to keeping your bird happy. Aim for a minimum of 18 x 18 x 18 inchs.

𝐏𝐞𝐫𝐜𝐡𝐞𝐬: Provide a variety of perches of different sizes and materials for foot health.

𝐓𝐨𝐲𝐬: Offer a bunch of different toys to make sure your birds aren't bored.

Diet

𝐒𝐞𝐞𝐝 𝐌𝐢𝐱: A good quality seed mix should be the main food source.

𝐅𝐫𝐞𝐬𝐡 𝐅𝐨𝐨𝐝𝐬: Offer fresh fruits and vegetables to make sure they get enough vitamins.

𝐖𝐚𝐭𝐞𝐫: Make sure you bird always have clean water!

𝐒𝐚𝐟𝐞 𝐄𝐧𝐯𝐢𝐫𝐨𝐧𝐦𝐞𝐧𝐭: Protect your budgie and keep it away from harm. Don't give it toxic substances or put them in extreme temperatures.

𝐒𝐨𝐜𝐢𝐚𝐥:Spend quality time with your budgie often but if you're too busy then consider buying it a friend.
